Gurbannazar Ezizov

Talented Turkmen Poet

Here's a timeline of Gurbannazar's life:

  • 1940 - Born in Buzmeyin district of Ashgabat City, Turkmenistan.

  • 1959 - Graduates from 29th Secondary School of Ashgabat City

  • 1964 - Graduates from Philology Faculty of Turkmen State University and starts working in United Press of Children's literature.

  • 1972 - After working for "Literature and Art" journal between 1965 to 1970, he starts working as consultant of Turkmen Writers Association until his death.

  • 1975 - Dies by shooting of Soviet soldier in highway near the Gazanjyk village of Balkan Region with poets Vasiliy Shabanov and Yuriy Rebinov
